how to use smartobjects

Step1

Open PSD. Select folder in PSD of desired phone color. Hide the one you the one you don’t need. Find layer named “Your design”. It’s a SmartObject.

Step2

Double-click or RMB “Edit Contents”. In opened window insert your image.

Step3

Close and save SmartObject window. Your image will be placed on Phone.
